Freight Forwarding & Logistics Services <

1. Freight Forwarding

🛫 Air Freight

Fast and reliable delivery for urgent or high-value shipments. Ideal for time-sensitive cargo requiring global connectivity.

⚓ Sea Freight

Economical and efficient bulk shipping across oceans using Full Container Load (FCL) or Less than Container Load (LCL) methods.

📦 Cargo Handling

Safe and systematic loading, unloading, and movement of cargo, ensuring zero damage and full compliance with handling norms.

🚛 Multimodal Transport

Integrated transport that combines air, sea, and road routes, offering a single-window solution for complex logistics needs.

2. Domestic Transport

🚚 Transport

General cargo movement across the country using various commercial vehicles for regular or ad-hoc delivery needs.

🛻 LCV (Light Commercial Vehicle)

Cost-effective solution for lighter shipments, intra-city or short-haul transport with flexibility and quicker dispatch.

🚛 FTL (Full Truck Load)

Dedicated truck space for large consignments, ensuring faster delivery with no intermediate stops or cross-loading.

🚛 Trailers

Ideal for bulky or long-length items, trailers support specialized cargo that doesn’t fit standard trucks.

🚜 ODC Movements (Over Dimensional Cargo)

Expert handling of cargo that exceeds regular dimensions — requiring permits, escorts, and route planning.

🚀 Express Cargo

Priority-based, high-speed delivery service designed for urgent consignments with guaranteed transit times.

❄️ Reefer Vehicles

Refrigerated trucks for temperature-sensitive goods like perishable food, pharma, or medical supplies.

3. Customs Clearance

📄 Normal / Second Hand

Clearance for standard imports or second-hand goods, ensuring compliance with duties, conditions, and DGFT regulations.

🏭 EOU / SEZ / FTWZ

Specialized clearance for goods under Export Oriented Units, Special Economic Zones, or Free Trade Warehousing Zones with unique tax and procedural frameworks.

💊 Pharma / Medical / FMCG

Fast-track clearances with documentation expertise for sensitive sectors like pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and consumer goods.

🏗️ Project Cargo

Handling of large-scale project equipment involving multiple containers, oversized cargo, and phased clearances.

4. Warehousing

🏢 Bonded / Private

Duty-deferred storage for imported goods in bonded warehouses or flexible private storage facilities for domestic stock.

🌐 FTWZ (Free Trade Warehousing Zone)

Specialized zones allowing storage and trade of imported goods without immediate customs clearance or duties.

📦 3PL (Third Party Logistics)

Outsourced inventory, warehousing, and fulfillment services — allowing businesses to scale without owning logistics assets.

❄️ Temperature Controlled

Storage facilities with regulated climate for perishable, medical, or chemical products needing strict temperature adherence.
